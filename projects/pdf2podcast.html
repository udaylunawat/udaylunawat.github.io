The Document to Audio Accessibility Challenge

Traditional reading workflows suffer from limited accessibility for visually impaired users, busy professionals, and multimodal learners who benefit from auditory content consumption. High-quality text-to-speech systems require sophisticated neural architectures and real-time processing capabilities that weren't previously available at scale.

PDF2Podcast bridges this gap by implementing cutting-edge TTS models (Kokoro and Kyutai) that transform static PDF documents into engaging, conversational audio experiences, making content universally accessible while maintaining the depth and context of the original material.
